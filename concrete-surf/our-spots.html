Amager Strandparken

Nothing like a sunny afternoon-session at this perfectly smooth concrete square surrounded by turquoise waters! Practice your longboard dancing steps and don't be shy - you'll always find some fellow longboarders here!

Superkilen Park

For everyone who doesn't want to leave the sparkling neighbourhoods of Norrebro for a quick longboarding break. Meet us at Superkilen Park to draw some dreamy lines on the red concrete. Beware of skateboarding teenagers though!

Værløse Airfield

If you have a car and time for a day trip, check out the abandoned airfield at Værløse, just 30 minutes outside of Copenhagen. You will find kilometres of smooth underground to cruise on just by yourself.
