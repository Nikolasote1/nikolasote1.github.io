About Concrete Surf

Life is more fun when you stand on a board.

Copenhagen is a paradise for longboarders. No hills, bike lanes everywhere and some of the smoothes pavements in the northern hemisphere make this city an asphalt eldorado. And what is better than surfing some concrete with like-minded people? We want to bring Copenhagen's longboarders together to create a vibrant community.
